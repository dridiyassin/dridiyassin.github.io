About PlanetImage

PlanetImage is a focused browser workspace for preparing images for websites, social networks, community apps and app stores. It combines resizing, cropping, compression and format conversion so the image only needs to be opened once.

Features

  • Custom dimensions and templates for popular platforms.
  • Crop-to-fill with zoom and draggable focus, fit-inside with a background color, or exact stretch.
  • Lossless PNG and adjustable JPG/WebP compression.
  • Automatic quality search for a maximum target file size.
  • Optional local smart upscaling with progressive resampling and edge-aware detail recovery.
  • Rotate, flip, prevent enlargement, before/result view and live size estimates.
  • Multi-image queue and batch processing with shared settings.
  • EXIF and GPS metadata removed during canvas export.

Privacy

Images are decoded, edited and encoded locally using your browser's Canvas API. They are never uploaded to us.
