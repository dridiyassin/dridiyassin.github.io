Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This policy applies to FreeToolPlanet and every tool published under it, including PlanetJSON, PlanetHTML, PlanetVid, PlanetTex, PlanetSVG and PlanetImage.

Files and content you process

Our tools are client-side applications. JSON, CSV, videos, audio, images, SVG artwork, icons, presets and generated textures are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded, stored or transmitted to our servers. We do not provide user accounts and do not collect the content you edit.

Tool-specific processing

  • PlanetJSON parses, compares, converts and generates code locally.
  • PlanetHTML reads selected project files into temporary browser memory, resolves local preview assets with temporary object URLs, and runs page previews inside a sandboxed frame.
  • PlanetVid uses FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly. Its runtime files are loaded from jsDelivr, while your media remains on your device.
  • PlanetTex generates textures locally with WebGL.
  • PlanetSVG builds vector artwork, transfers it between its Editor and Animator through local browser storage, and exports static or animated SVG locally.
  • PlanetImage uses the browser Canvas API. Re-encoded exports do not retain source EXIF or embedded GPS metadata.

Hosting and third-party resources

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log technical information such as IP addresses for security and operational purposes under the GitHub Privacy Statement. Pages load fonts from Google Fonts. PlanetVid loads its browser FFmpeg runtime from jsDelivr.

Advertising and cookies

Advertisements are served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted. You can manage personalization in Google Ads Settings and learn how Google uses data from partner sites in Google's partner-sites policy. Visitors in regions such as the EEA and UK may be shown a consent message.

Analytics

FreeToolPlanet does not currently run its own analytics. If this changes, this policy will be updated.
