About PlanetVid

PlanetVid is a free, browser-based video converter. It converts videos to MP4, WebM or animated GIF and extracts MP3/WAV audio - with trimming, resizing, quality control and muting - without ever uploading your file.

Under the hood it runs FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly directly in your browser tab. The site itself is a set of static files: there is no server, no account and no watermark, and a private video stays private on your device.

Features

  • Convert to MP4 (H.264 + AAC) or WebM (VP8 + Vorbis).
  • Turn clips into animated GIFs with selectable frame rate.
  • Extract the audio track as MP3 or WAV.
  • Trim by start time and duration, resize to 1080p/720p/480p/360p, remove audio, pick a quality level.
  • Live progress bar and full FFmpeg log.
  • Accepts MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, WebM and most other formats FFmpeg can read.
